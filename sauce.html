IMAGE SOURCE FINDERS


If you want to find the artist of an anime-style artwork, or search for artworks drawn by the same artist, you can use the following websites:


http://iqdb.org/ for standalone anime-style artwork.


https://saucenao.com/ can search for screenshots of anime/hentai and manga/hentai manga pages, as well as most things that iqdb can.


https://tineye.com/ is used mainly for searching images that have been cropped or are otherwise incomplete.


https://ascii2d.net/ is especially suitable for finding originals from Twitter. The "特徴検索" button next to your image usually gives better results.


https://images.google.com/ desktop mode, as a global fallback if the above methods do not work.





ARTWORK DISCOVERY


For discovering new artwork, and for searching for an image's source without the original image:

https://www.pixiv.net/

https://pawoo.net/

https://sp.nijie.info/

https://sp.seiga.nicovideo.jp/illust

https://twitter.com/

These websites are usually the original sources of most artwork, search these if you know what you're doing.


If your Japanese is lacking:


https://danbooru.donmai.us/ is usually the best resource as it is very well tagged, is of a high quality and has no ads, cons are that 1. it automatically filters some tags and 2. it limits you to two tags per search which is very restrictive at times.


https://gelbooru.com/ is less complete and has more ads, but has the ability to show all tags and doesn't restrict you to two tags.


http://konachan.com/ is used mainly for finding wallpaper-quality artwork.


https://yande.re/ only hosts artwork with a high resolution and a high quality; use this website for an extreme high-quality guarantee.


A few hints on how to use the search systems on Danbooru and other boorus:

1. Spacebar always starts a new tag. If you want to search for a term that contains more than two words [e.g. Misaka Mikoto], use lowercase letters and an underscore instead of a space [e.g. misaka_mikoto]. Some characters might have a longer tag with the series name behind it [e.g. shimakaze_(kantai_collection)] due to other characters sharing the same name.

2. A minus in front of a tag removes all of that tag from the search results; if you don't want yuri in your Mikoto art results, search "misaka_mikoto -yuri".

3. Some special tags that may be of particular interest to you:

"order:score" orders the results from the artwork with the highest to the one with the lowest score.

"rating:s" returns safe for work artwork. (You can use Safebooru if you only want safe art.) Naturally, "-rating:s" returns NSFW art.





HENTAI MANGA AND TRANSLATIONS


https://e-hentai.org/

The big daddy of all hentai artwork sites. Has a bland interface but is also the most complete and has no ads; also hosts things like R18 cosplay and non-hentai manga, as well as some artwork that would otherwise be paid content only. Filters some tags, the "hidden" website https://exhentai.org/ (aka sadpanda) does not. You need to log in to the forums (and possibly wait a week) to access sadpanda. An archive of deleted uploads can be found at panda.chaika.moe.


https://nhentai.net/

The plebian version of e-hentai. Main features are image preloading and (generally) faster speeds. Annoying ads, bring your own adblocker. The "default" website everyone uses when they mention hentai; the 6 (or sometimes 5) digit numbers you see on /r/animemes and other places refer to the manga IDs on this website.





ANIMATED HENTAI AND OTHERS


https://sukebei.nyaa.si/

This is the one-stop shop for downloading hentai anime via torrents, as streaming websites tend to be unreliable and may interrupt a session with buffering. Sukebei also has a sizeable stash of JAV and hentai manga.


https://ecchi.iwara.tv/ contains MMD and other related videos.


For other purposes, such as non-R18 anime, manga, music, and Weeb Stuff in general: https://nyaa.si/ and a torrent client such as qBittorent is your best option for most purposes.





PAYING FOR ARTWORK


https://www.melonbooks.co.jp/

https://www.toranoana.jp/

The two big shops to purchase actual manga and R18 manga from, along with other weeb items; use these or any website the artist links on pixiv/twitter/etc. if you want to buy hard copies.

http://www.amazon.jp/ also has plenty of stuff if you know how to navigate it.


https://fanbox.cc/

https://fantia.jp/

https://enty.jp/

Soft copy purchasing and subscription websites for individual artists.

Patreon also has some artwork that usually leans more "western" in style.


http://skeb.jp/

If you have the money to actually commission an artist to draw something for you, this is the website.





UNCENSORED ARTWORK

(borrowed from the excellent /r/wholesomehentai's wiki's licensed guide)


If the hentai manga that you're reading is from this range of magazine issues, then there most probably exists a uncensored (and English translated) version by FAKKU:


  • COMIC Kairakuten: 100% licensed from issue 2015-06 onwards.
  • COMIC X-Eros: 100% licensed from issue #30 onwards.
  • COMIC Shitsurakuten: 100% licensed from issue 2016-04 onwards.
  • COMIC Kairakuten BEAST: 100% licensed from issue 2016-12 onwards.
  • COMIC Bavel: 100% licensed from issue 2017-06 onwards.
  • COMIC Europa: 100% licensed from vol.11 onwards.
  • Girls forM: Issues 13-16 are licensed.
  • COMIC HAPPINING: 100% licensed.
  • COMIC Aoha: 100% licensed.
  • COMIC Weekly Kairakuten: 100% licensed.
  • Dascomi: 100% licensed.
  • COMIC Koh: vol.1-2 are licensed.


Pirated versions of these manga are not linked to here, as websites that host them have a tendency of disappearing without warning. For these manga, it may be easier to Google search the name and artist if they cannot be found at the websites provided.

Other individual tankoubon or even doujinshi may also have uncensored versions as well. Additionally, e-hentai also hosts artwork individually decensored by third party fan editors.

Additionally, if you can read traditional Chinese, https://future-digi.com/ sells physical doujinshi in traditional Chinese from Taiwan; search 無修正 for uncensored hard copies.
