3rd Kaguya-sama Q&A Translations

Satorin


Q.001 What do you keep in mind [the most] when you draw manga?

> To make the development of the manga as realistic as I can.


Q.002 So far, the only StuCo member who hasn't fallen in love is Fujiwara Chika-chan, but will youth ever come for Chika-chan?

> Fujiwara's always the most youthful.


Q.003 Have we done the other side of the Cubari Facaccimo before?

> You won't be able to come back if you go searching for things about the Cubari Faccacimo.


Q.004 Do Shirogane and Kaguya normally wear their matching necklaces to school?

> Seems like they're wearing them secretly.


Q.005 What happened to the game of tag between Secretary Fujiwara and Hayasaka-san (in Vol.16, chapter 152)? Their relationship seemed to revert back to normal during the school trip.

> Fujiwara managed to escape. Fujiwara seems to be a little wary of Hayasaka now.


Q.006 Maki once called herself "a legitimate descendant of the Shinomiya bloodline", but given the relationship between the Shinomiyas and the Shijos, why does she emphasise her connection to the Shinomiya family?

> It seems that she sees the current Shinomiya family as being illegitimate.


Q.007 It seems that the worldviews of Kaguya-sama and Oshi no Ko are connected, but chronologically speaking, when does Kaguya-sama take place in relation to Oshi no Ko?

> Oshi no Ko is chronologically set years after Kaguya-sama. It seems that the members of the StuCo will then be members of society.


Q.008 It seems that Hayasaka's birthday has not been officially published, but when is her birthday officially?

> It seems that her grade in school is a year off from her actual birthday.


Q.009 I have a question about Kei-chan. I think Kei-chan passed the entrance examination [for Shuchiin] after Prez failed both the kindergarten and primary school entrance examinations, but did she start from kindergarten or from primary school? I would also like to know how she met the Fujiwara sisters.

> It seems that many who started from kindergarten are considered to be at the top of of Shuchiin. Kei-chan and Ishigami seem to have started from elementary school.


Q.010 Will Maki-chan ever find happiness?

> I'm sure she'll be rich.


Q.011 (In Volume 23, Chapter 222), Fujiwara challenged Ice Kaguya to a game, but who won that game?

> Kaguya won the game itself, but Fujiwara seems to have won overall. It seems that Kaguya can't beat Fujiwara very often.


Q.012 Where do these vignettes that make one laugh and cry come from?

> From not lying about emotions.
